Message from Superintendent

Banas Medical College and Research Institute, Palanpur was proposed in the year 2017 under Public Private Project from Govt of Gujarat. Palanpur civil hospital, associated as teaching hospital of BMCRI provides comprehensive health care to the population of Palanpur and surrounding area of North Gujarat under one roof.

BMCRI is a brain child of members of Galbabhai Nanjibhai Patel Charitable trust who has proved their excellence in establishing Asia’s No. 1 Banas dairy.

The main motto of this teaching hospital is to cater health care services to community with love and at affordable cost. As per availability of primary facility this hospital is providing health care support to almost 90% population of Banaskantha and surrounding area of North Gujarat.

Presently the patient annual turnover figure touches 600 outpatients and 200 Inpatients which includes 6000 surgeries per year. As a collaborative project of Banas Dairy, Palanpur and Govt of Gujarat, people’s optimism towards health care in North Gujarat has increased.
