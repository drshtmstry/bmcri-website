Department of Pediatrics

The Department of Pediatrics is dedicated to providing treatment to all pediatric patients from birth to 18 years of age. We believe in group dynamics and work integrated with different departments of the institute to provide comprehensive care.

Vision

To provide comprehensive fundamental knowledge of Pediatrics to undergraduate students, helping them understand the basics of medical conditions, prevention, and appropriate treatment for the pediatric age group.

Facilities Overview

Situated in the "D" block of Civil Hospital, the department operates four units comprising specialized clinics, critical care units, and wards spread across multiple floors to ensure holistic child healthcare.

Infrastructure & Services

OPD & Clinics

Located on the Ground Floor, including two general OPD rooms, one immunization clinic, and a special clinic room running on different weekdays.

Critical Care

Advanced care facilities including an SNCU on the 1st floor, a designated Isolation room, and a Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU).

Wards & Academics

Three pediatric wards located on the 3rd floor for inpatient care, supported by a departmental library and faculty offices.
