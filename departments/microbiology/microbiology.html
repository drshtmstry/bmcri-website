Department of Microbiology

The Microbiology department is managed by a team of qualified & dedicated faculty and laboratory technicians. The department is fully committed to provide high-end teaching, training, and diagnostic services, and create a conducive environment for research and training of paramedical staff as per the vision and mission of the Institute's management.

Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence for Microbiology Practice, Education, Research & Clinical Service Provider.

Mission

To provide high-end teaching, training, and diagnostic services, and create a conducive environment for research and training of paramedical staff.

Clinical Services

Central Laboratory

Equipped with diagnostic facilities for Bacteriology, Serology, Virology, Parasitology, Mycology, & Mycobacteriology.

Molecular Lab

Designated Molecular (RT-PCR) laboratory for providing essential Molecular services including COVID-19 and Hepatitis B & C Viral Load testing.

National Programs

Active participation in NVHCP, ICTC (HIV), RNTCP (TB), and IDSP programs of the Government of Gujarat.

Activities

  • Participating in Hospital Infection & Prevention Control program.
  • Plays a crucial role in the Antibiotic Stewardship Program for the institute.
  • Participating in EQAS programme conducted by IAMM.

Teaching Philosophy

The curriculum is developed as per National Medical Commission norms to provide understanding of the natural history of infectious diseases.

  • Interactive lectures, small group discussions & tutorials.
  • Self-directed learning, seminars & integrated teaching.
  • Hands-on practical sessions and demonstrations.

Facilities

  • Well-established practical laboratories.
  • Well-equipped demonstration rooms.
  • Departmental library with >100 books.
  • Dedicated Research Laboratory.
  • Museum with mounted specimens & charts.
