Department of General Medicine

The Department of General Medicine is the backbone of the hospital, providing comprehensive care for a wide range of adult diseases. We are dedicated to excellence in patient care, medical education, and clinical research.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Internal Medicine, providing evidence-based, holistic, and compassionate care to patients while fostering an environment of continuous learning and research for future physicians.

Mission

To provide high-quality diagnostic and therapeutic services to the community. To train undergraduate and postgraduate students in the art and science of medicine, emphasizing ethical practice and empathy.

Infrastructure & Services

OPD Services

Daily Outpatient Department (OPD) services run by experienced physicians, catering to a high volume of patients with diverse medical conditions.

Inpatient Wards

Well-equipped male and female wards providing round-the-clock monitoring and care for patients requiring hospitalization.

ICCU & Critical Care

State-of-the-art Intensive Coronary Care Unit (ICCU) and Medical ICU facilities for managing critical and life-threatening emergencies.

Academic Focus

  • Regular clinical case discussions and seminars.
  • Training in management of infectious and non-communicable diseases.
  • Bedside clinics for skill development.

Patient Care

We focus on holistic recovery and management of chronic conditions.

  • Diabetes and Hypertension clinics.
  • Infectious disease management.
  • Geriatric care services.

Facilities

  • ECG and 2D-Echo facilities.
  • Pulmonary Function Test (PFT).
  • Departmental Library.
