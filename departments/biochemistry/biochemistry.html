Department of Biochemistry

The Department of Biochemistry bridges the gap between fundamental science and clinical medicine. We are committed to equipping students with the ability to diagnose diseases through the analysis and interpretation of clinical investigations.

Vision

To give detailed knowledge of human biochemistry to 1st-year MBBS students, helping them correlate clinical biochemistry with modern medicine. This ensures that an Indian Medical Graduate (IMG) will be able to diagnose different diseases based on the analysis and interpretation of various laboratory investigations.

Mission

To provide diagnostic services at the Central Biochemistry Laboratory for the general hospital, enhancing the health matrix of the Banaskantha district. We also aim to enhance student learning through elective clinical postings and provide hands-on experience to interns for their future clinical practice.

Key Services & Training

Central Biochemistry Lab

Providing essential diagnostic services to various patients of the General Hospital, Palanpur, serving the Banaskantha district.

Student Clinical Posting

Enhancing MBBS learning by giving detailed exposure during elective clinical postings at the clinical biochemistry laboratory.

Internship Program

Providing hands-on experience to interns during rotatory internship postings to apply concepts in future clinical practice.

Objectives

  • Correlate importance of clinical biochemistry in modern medicine.
  • Diagnose diseases based on lab investigation analysis.
  • Apply laboratory medicine concepts in clinical practice.

Academic Focus

The curriculum is designed to ensure students understand the chemical basis of life and disease.

  • Detailed knowledge of Human Biochemistry for 1st Year MBBS.
  • Elective clinical postings for practical exposure.
  • Rotatory internship in Laboratory Medicine.

Facilities

  • Central Biochemistry Laboratory.
  • Student Practical Laboratories.
  • Demonstration Rooms.
  • Departmental Library.
