Department of Psychiatry

The Department of Psychiatry is dedicated to leading efforts in understanding, diagnosing, treating, and preventing psychiatric disorders. We aim to provide state-of-the-art care and direction to improve community behavioral health.

Vision

To prepare future clinicians and provide state-of-the-art care. We strive to lead the effort to understand and prevent psychiatric disorders while giving direction to the community to improve psychological health.

Mission

To provide distinctive patient care with clinical excellence and deliver quality mental healthcare services, specifically reaching the underprivileged sections of society while adhering to teaching hospital compliances.

Inpatient Care (IPD)

The department operates a dedicated unit with 20 beds, segregated into 10 beds for male patients and 10 beds for female patients.

Clinical Services

Includes Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT), De-addiction Services, Psychological Assessment, Psychotherapy, and Disability Certifications (Wed/Fri).

Specialized Care

  • De-addiction Services: Comprehensive support for recovery.
  • Consultation Liaison: Integrated care with other departments.
  • Disability Certification: Available on Wednesdays & Fridays.

Therapies Offered

We provide a range of therapeutic interventions tailored to patient needs.

  • Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT).
  • Psychological Assessment.
  • Individual & Group Psychotherapy.

Community Focus

  • Reaching underprivileged sections of society.
  • Preparing future clinicians.
  • Adhering to teaching hospital compliances.
