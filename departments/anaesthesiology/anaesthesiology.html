Department of Anaesthesiology

The Department of Anaesthesiology is the lifeline of the surgical wing, dedicated to ensuring patient safety and comfort during surgical procedures. We provide comprehensive perioperative care, critical care services, and pain management.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in perioperative medicine and critical care. We aim to adopt the latest advancements in anesthesia safety, monitoring, and pain relief to ensure the highest standards of patient care.

Mission

To provide safe and effective anesthesia services for all surgical specialties. To train competent anesthesiologists capable of managing critical emergencies and to provide compassionate pain management services.

Infrastructure & Services

Operation Theatres

State-of-the-art modular operation theatres equipped with advanced workstations, multipara monitors, and ventilators for major surgeries.

Critical Care

Managing the Surgical Intensive Care Unit (SICU) with round-the-clock monitoring and ventilatory support for critically ill patients.

PAC & Pain Clinic

Dedicated Pre-Anaesthetic Checkup (PAC) clinic for patient optimization and a Pain Management clinic for chronic pain relief.

Clinical Scope

  • General & Regional Anaesthesia.
  • Labour Analgesia & Obstetric Anaesthesia.
  • Paediatric & Geriatric Anaesthesia.

Academic Activities

Focusing on skill acquisition and emergency management.

  • BLS & ACLS Training Workshops.
  • Simulation-based learning.
  • Case discussions & Morbidity meetings.

Facilities

  • High-end Ventilators & Infusion Pumps.
  • Video Laryngoscopes & Fibreoptic Bronchoscopes.
  • Departmental Library.
