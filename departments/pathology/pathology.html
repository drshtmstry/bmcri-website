Department of Pathology

The Department of Pathology is the bridge between clinical medicine and basic science. We are dedicated to linking etiology and pathogenesis to provide optimal patient care while excelling in education, research, and diagnostics.

Vision

To provide the best possible education to medical students, provide high-quality diagnostic services to patients, and promote research in the subject of pathology.

Mission

To produce doctors able to link disease pathogenesis with patient care. We aim to excel in diagnosis and education by continuously upgrading staff knowledge through recent advances and self-improvement.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Diagnostic Labs

Air-conditioned Histopathology & Cytopathology labs with fully automated machines, grossing stations with camera recording, and standardized staining units.

Advanced Microscopy

Equipped with Decahead, Pentahead, and Trinocular microscopes interfaced with TV screens for superior teaching and high-quality diagnostics.

Academic Resources

A fully developed museum with 309 mounted specimens, an air-conditioned library, and a dedicated research laboratory.

24/7 Clinical Services

Round-the-clock functioning Blood Bank and Central Laboratory ensuring uninterrupted patient care and diagnostic support.

Educational Focus

  • Linking etiology and pathogenesis of disease.
  • Application of knowledge for optimal patient care.
  • Upgradation of knowledge using recent advances.

Teaching Facilities

Designed to provide an optimal working and learning environment.

  • Two Demonstration rooms with Audio-Visual facilities.
  • Practical Laboratory with Binocular microscopes for students.
  • Air-conditioned chambers for teaching staff.

Lab Equipment

  • Automated Tissue Processing Units.
  • Microscopes with Camera Connectivity.
  • Grossing station with recording facility.
  • 309 Mounted Museum Specimens.
