Department of Dermatology

The Dermatology Department aspires to be a center of excellence, delivering high-quality education, research, and compassionate patient care. We are committed to extending patient-centered care and ensuring the highest standards of dermatological health.

Vision

To be at the forefront of dermatological advancements, we aim to develop infrastructure equipped with lasers for scar/hair/tattoo removal, phototherapy units, and radiofrequency. We envision specialty clinics for leprosy, vitiligo, psoriasis, and vesiculobullous diseases.

Mission

To provide comprehensive education to medical students and residents while delivering holistic, evidence-based treatment to patients. We strive to promote skin health awareness through community outreach and inter-departmental collaboration.

Inpatient Care

Located in the B Block, the department provides indoor patient facilities with a total capacity of 10 beds (5 Male + 5 Female).

Advanced Care

Focus on specialty clinics for Leprosy, Vitiligo, and Psoriasis, alongside procedural dermatology including phototherapy and radiofrequency treatments.

Academic Goals

  • Comprehensive education for students & residents.
  • Equipping students with skills to excel in dermatology.
  • Fostering innovation and dermatological knowledge.

Patient Care

Committed to compassionate, patient-centered care with a holistic approach.

  • Evidence-based treatment & proper diagnosis.
  • Addressing dermatological needs of the community.
  • Improving quality of life through better skin health.

Future Facilities

  • Lasers for Scar, Hair, & Tattoo removal.
  • Phototherapy Unit for Vitiligo.
  • Radiofrequency for dermatological causes.
