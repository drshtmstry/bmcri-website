Department of General Surgery

The Department of General Surgery is committed to providing affordable, latest surgical treatments in an environment of compassion. We focus on minimally invasive options to ensure early recovery and less pain for our patients.

Vision

To promote laparoscopic surgery from its present status to advanced procedures and establish a state-of-the-art endoscopy unit. We aim to start Post Graduate and Super-speciality programs as early as possible.

Teaching Concept

To provide integrated knowledge and surgical skills to students based on anatomy, physiology, and pathology. We emphasize self-learning, moral standards, and the application of theoretical knowledge in patient care.

Infrastructure & Facilities

OPD & Academic Block

Located on the Ground Floor of 'B' Block. Includes HOD office and Teaching room situated next to the OPD for efficient clinical teaching.

Operation Theatres

Modular Surgical OT in 'D' block equipped with laparoscopic instruments. Includes separate Minor OTs in the OPD and near the Emergency/Trauma ward.

Surgical ICU

A dedicated Surgical Intensive Care Unit situated near the Emergency ward on the ground floor of 'D' block for critical patient management.

Future Developments

Planning and construction are underway for 3 additional modular OTs and a dedicated endoscopy room for diagnostic and therapeutic procedures.

Academic Focus

  • Integrated teaching based on new NMC curriculum.
  • Continuous evaluation of learning processes.
  • Inculcation of ethical and moral standards.

Clinical Services

Focused on compassion and patient satisfaction.

  • Minimally invasive surgical options.
  • Advanced laparoscopic surgical procedures.
  • Endoscopic diagnostic & therapeutic procedures.

Resources

  • Central Library with 42 types of Surgery books.
  • 10 types of Journals of Surgery available.
  • Dedicated teaching rooms for daily exposure.
