Obstetrics & Gynaecology

The Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology is dedicated to the health and well-being of women through all stages of life. We provide comprehensive care ranging from antenatal supervision to complex gynaecological surgeries.

Vision

To be a premier center for women's health, providing safe maternity services and advanced gynaecological care. We aim to excel in high-risk pregnancy management and minimally invasive surgery while training compassionate future specialists.

Mission

To reduce maternal and neonatal mortality through dedicated care. To provide affordable, evidence-based treatment for gynaecological disorders and to promote reproductive health awareness in the community.

Infrastructure & Services

Maternity Services

24/7 Labor Room facilities with attached Septic Labor Room and Eclampsia Room. Specialized care for High-Risk Pregnancies.

Gynaecology

Comprehensive care for menstrual disorders, infertility, and gynaecological cancers. Advanced facilities for Laparoscopic and Hysteroscopic surgeries.

Family Welfare

Integrated Family Planning services, Cancer Screening (Pap Smear, VIA/VILI), and Menopause clinics.

Clinical Procedures

  • Normal & Assisted Vaginal Deliveries.
  • Caesarean Sections & Hysterectomies.
  • Ultrasonography & Colposcopy.

Academic Activities

We emphasize clinical skills and emergency obstetric care.

  • Maternal Mortality & Morbidity Audits.
  • Labor Room drills & Skill Lab training.
  • Post-graduate seminars & Case discussions.

Facilities

  • Antenatal & Postnatal Wards.
  • Post-operative Recovery Room.
  • Departmental Library.
