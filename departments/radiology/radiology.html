Department of Radio-Diagnosis

The Department of Radio-Diagnosis is the diagnostic hub of the institute, equipped with state-of-the-art imaging technologies. We provide 24/7 diagnostic and interventional services to facilitate accurate and timely medical treatment.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in diagnostic imaging and interventional radiology. We aim to integrate advanced technology with clinical expertise to provide precise diagnoses and foster research in imaging sciences.

Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and safe imaging services to all patients. To train future radiologists in modern imaging modalities and radiation safety protocols while maintaining the highest ethical standards.

Infrastructure & Modalities

X-Ray & Fluoroscopy

Digital Radiography (DR) systems and Fluoroscopy units for high-resolution skeletal, chest, and contrast studies with minimal radiation exposure.

CT & MRI

Advanced Multi-slice CT scanners for rapid trauma imaging and high-field MRI for detailed neuro, musculoskeletal, and body imaging.

Ultrasound & Doppler

High-end Ultrasound machines for antenatal, abdominal, and soft tissue imaging, including Color Doppler for vascular studies.

Clinical Services

  • 24/7 Emergency Imaging Services.
  • Interventional Radiology procedures (Biopsies, Drainages).
  • Mammography for Breast Cancer Screening.

Academic Activities

We emphasize radiological physics and clinical correlation.

  • Daily Film Reading Sessions.
  • Inter-departmental Clinical Meetings.
  • Radiation Safety Training.

Facilities

  • PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication System).
  • Reporting Rooms with High-Res Monitors.
  • Departmental Library & E-Learning resources.
