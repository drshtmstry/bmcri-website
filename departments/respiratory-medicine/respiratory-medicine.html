Respiratory Medicine

The Department of Respiratory Medicine is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases of the respiratory system. We play a pivotal role in national health programs and medical education.

Vision

To be a premier center for respiratory care, providing advanced clinical services including ICU admissions and specialized procedures, while fostering a strong academic environment for future doctors.

Mission

To provide comprehensive lectures and clinical teaching to MBBS students and interns. We are committed to coordinating the National Tuberculosis Elimination Programme (NTEP) and offering robust OPD and indoor patient services.

Facilities & Services

Academic Training

Conducting lectures and clinical teaching for MBBS students regarding Respiratory Medicine. Specialized training for intern doctors during their posting.

Clinical Care

Comprehensive patient care including OPD services, Indoor patient services, and specialized ICU admission for critical respiratory cases.

NTEP Coordination

Serving as a nodal center for the National Tuberculosis Elimination Programme (NTEP), managing TB cases and coordination activities.

Clinical Procedures

We perform various specialized procedures related to the Respiratory Medicine subject.

  • Diagnostic Pleural Procedures.
  • Pulmonary Function Testing.
  • Critical Care Management.

Student Training

Focused on clinical exposure and theoretical knowledge.

  • Bedside clinical teaching for MBBS.
  • OPD service training for Interns.
  • Case discussions and seminars.

Infrastructure

  • Respiratory Medicine OPD.
  • Indoor Wards.
  • Respiratory ICU.
  • Tuberculosis Clinic (NTEP).
