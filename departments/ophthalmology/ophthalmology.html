Department of Ophthalmology

We are committed to providing the best services in the treatment of eye diseases and disorders. Our mission is to offer a complete range of examination, treatment, and surgical options utilizing the latest technologies.

Vision

To be the leader in innovation of patient care in ophthalmology and deliver superior quality programs. We aim to develop fully functional Retinal, Glaucoma, and Cornea units, along with a functional eye bank.

Mission

To provide a complete range of surgical options utilizing the latest technologies. Our ophthalmologists are dedicated to specific treatments for each patient, ensuring the highest quality of service.

Services & Facilities

Clinical Services

General Ophthalmology, Vision Tests, Glaucoma Treatment, and School Health Check-ups. Offering SICS and Phacoemulsification for cataract surgery.

Advanced Equipment

Equipped with Auto Refractometer, Non-contact Tonometer, A-scan Ultrasonography, Surgical Microscopes, and Nd-Yag Laser machine.

Emergency & Community

24/7 Emergency care for trauma (RTA, corneal tear). Functional eye collection center and regular eye camps in nearby villages.
