Department of Orthopaedics

The Department of Orthopaedics is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation, and prevention of injuries and diseases of the musculoskeletal system. We are committed to providing world-class care for trauma, joint disorders, and spinal conditions.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in musculoskeletal care, providing advanced surgical interventions like arthroplasty and arthroscopy, while training the next generation of surgeons with high ethical standards.

Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality trauma and orthopaedic care to all patients. To integrate modern technology with compassionate care and promote research in the field of orthopaedic surgery.

Infrastructure & Services

Trauma & Emergency

24/7 dedicated trauma services equipped to handle complex fractures, poly-trauma cases, and emergency orthopaedic procedures.

Surgical Facilities

Modular Operation Theatres equipped with C-Arm image intensifiers, Arthroscopy units, and specialized instrumentation for Joint Replacement.

Rehabilitation

Integrated Physiotherapy unit providing post-operative care, mobilization therapy, and long-term rehabilitation support.

Clinical Services

  • Joint Replacement Surgery (Hip & Knee).
  • Arthroscopy & Sports Medicine.
  • Spine Surgery & Paediatric Orthopaedics.

Academic Activities

We focus on hands-on skill acquisition and theoretical knowledge.

  • Daily Trauma Meetings & Case Discussions.
  • Surgical Skills Workshops (Bone fixation, Suturing).
  • Research publications and journal clubs.

Facilities

  • Dedicated Orthopaedic Wards (Male/Female).
  • Plaster Room & Dressing Room.
  • Departmental Library with latest journals.
